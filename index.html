About Us

Handcrafted treasures from the heart of Keeranatham, Coimbatore

Our Story

Kai Vannam Creations was born from a passion for traditional Tamil craftsmanship. Every product is carefully handcrafted using natural materials — each basket, bag, and wire creation carries the warmth and care of skilled hands.

Located in Keeranatham, Coimbatore, we bring you authentic handmade fruit baskets, woven bags, black rope bags, and beautiful wire art — perfect for gifting, home décor, and everyday use.

We believe in sustainable, meaningful making. No two pieces are identical — each one is unique, just like the person who owns it.
